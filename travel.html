Travel

How to get there

Travel Directions

From London:
  1. Come off the m25 at junction 28 for the A12
  2. Stay on the A12 north through Chelmsford & Colchester
  3. At Ipswitch, where the A12 joins the A14 (Toys R us roundabout) - follow the A14 west towards Bury St. Edmonds and Norwich
  4. Exit the A14 at junction 51 and follow the A140 North towards Diss and Norwich
  5. Follow the A140 for approx. 11 miles, then turn right onto the B1117 (well signposted but opposite "The Auberge") towards Eye and Stradbrooke.
  6. In Stradbroke, turn left onto the B1118. This junction is opposite the White Hart pub and war memorial on the corner in Stradbroke.
  7. Continue to follow the directions from Stradbroke below ...


From Stradbroke:
  1. In Stradbroke, turn left onto the B1118. This junction is opposite the White Hart pub and war memorial on the corner in Stradbroke.
  2. You will follow this road for about 1.5 miles. Almost immediately after a right turn signposted Wingfield and Fressingfield, take a right turn signposted Wingfield green and Syleham
  3. You will shortly come to Corner Green Farm on your left (large green barns) and a sign for "Wingfield 30mph"
  4. Immediately afterwards on the left, there is a small lane with a footpath sign on the near corner and phone pole on the far corner. This is a private road, which leads to Warrens Farmhouse
  5. Warrens Farmhouse is the first house you come to - There will be signs and balloons on the way down the path on the way.
