Some places to stay in Suffolk

These are just some of the options for accommodation for the weekend. Being the middle of summer, the hotels do book up quickly so we advise that you get something booked as far in advance as you can. We reccommend looking on hotels.com, expedia & Airbnb.



Wingfield Escapes

Wingfield Escapes was created to provide luxury glamping accommodation for those who just want to relax and unwind whether it be enjoying country walks, delicious food & a drink at the local pubs or exploring the local art and history.


Best Western Brome Grange

Best Western Brome Grange Hotel is perfectly perched on the Norfolk / Suffolk border, in the village of Brome. Wrapped up in 4 acres of grounds, you'll be hidden away between the cities of Norwich and Ipswich, with some of England's most superb scenery within easy reach.


The White Horse Inn

We are an independent Inn run by the same family for over 15 years, offering homemade quality food in a friendly and comfortable environment.
