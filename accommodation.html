Rest your weary head

Some places to stay in York

There are just some of the options for accommodation for the weekend. Being the middle of summer, the hotels do book up quickly so we advise that you get something booked as far in advance as you can


The Grand York

Opposite the original city walls, this sophisticated Edwardian hotel is set in the former headquarters of the North Eastern Railway, and was built in 1906. It's 4 minutes' walk from York train station.


Hotel Ibis York

This casual budget hotel in a former townhouse is an 8-minute walk from York train station and a 5-minute walk from Mickelgate, the famous medieval gateway into York.


Park Inn by Radisson

On the banks of the River Ouse, this modern hotel is an 8-minute walk from York Theatre Royal and an 11-minute walk from York Minster.


Hampton by Hilton

An 8-minute walk from York railway station, this informal hotel is also 14 minutes' walk from the National Railway Museum and 12 minutes away on foot from York Castle Museum.


Premier Inn York City Centre

This modern budget hotel is set a 5-minute walk from York train station and an 11-minute walk from Jorvik Viking Centre.
